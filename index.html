GitHub account of Quoll42

Why Quoll42?  A quoll is a cat-like Australian marsupial that I choose for my GitHub username a long while back, because it is distinctly Australian and this one has a great pair of programming hands. 42 is of course the Answer to the Ultimate Question of Life, the Universe, and Everything, as calculated by the supercomputer Deep Thought over a period of 7.5 million years.
